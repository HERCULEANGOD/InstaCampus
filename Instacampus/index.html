About INSTACAMPUS

INSTACAMPUS is a centralized platform for university students, faculty, and clubs to stay connected and engaged. With real-time updates on events, notifications from clubs and departments, and direct communication channels, INSTACAMPUS simplifies campus-wide communications.

Key Features

  • Instant updates on events and club activities
  • Direct messaging with faculty and club representatives
  • Notifications for important announcements
  • User-friendly design with mobile support

How It Works

Log in with your university credentials to access your personalized dashboard. From there, explore events, stay informed, and communicate with peers and faculty.

  • Create an account or log in with your university credentials.
  • Receive updates on events, announcements, and more.
  • Connect and interact with campus clubs, faculty, and fellow students.
